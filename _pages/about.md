Welcome! I am a Finance PhD student and Robert James Waller Doctoral Fellow at the Kelley School of Business, Indiana University. My research interest lies in corporate finance, with a focus on how information frictions, labor market dynamics, and technological change shape firm behavior and organizational outcomes. I hold a Bachelor's in Business Administration and Financial Engineering from Korea University, as well as a Master’s in Finance from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

Research Interests: Corporate Finance · Labor and Finance · Organizational Economics · Innovation
