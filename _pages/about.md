My research primarily focuses on corporate finance and its intersections with labor and organizational economics. I hold a B.B.A. in Business Administration and Financial Engineering from Korea University and an M.S. in Finance from the University of Illinois.

*Research Interests:* Corporate Finance · Labor and Finance · Financial Intermediation · Organizational Economics
